A Brief Introduction

I am currently a Ph.D. student at Satellite Research Center in School of Electrical and Electronic Engineering at Nanyang Technological University (NTU), Singapore. My project is on developing "High accuracy star trackers for nano-satellites" under the guidance of Assistant Professor Shoushun Chen .

I completed my under graduation in Electrical and Electronic Engineering (EEE) from National Institute of Technology (NIT), Tiruchirappalli, India [2010-2014]. Immediately after completing my under graduation, I joined as a Ph.D. student at NTU, Singapore in August 2014. I am currently in the third year of my Ph.D. journey.

If you are more interested in my undertaken projects so far, you are welcomed to take a look at the Projects Tab. More information about my current research interest and academic achievements so far can be found in the About Tab. My publications so far have been listed in the Publications Tab. Apart from my research, I have several other activities that interest me and make my world, which can be found in the Leisure Tab.
